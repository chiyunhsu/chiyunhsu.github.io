Tentative plan
Week 1-3: Automorphic forms of GL(1) - Dirichlet characters, Dirichlet L-functions, analytic continuation and functional equation. Adelic language, Fourier analysis and Tate's thesis.
Week 4-10: Automorphic forms of GL(2) - Modular forms, L-functions and properties. Automorphic forms and representations. Local theory. Rankin-Selberg method and converse theorem.
