I am a Hedrick Assistant Adjunct Professor at UCLA. I received my PhD from Harvard University in 2019 under the supervision of of Prof. Barry Mazur.

My research interest is in Number Theory. I am particularly interested in p-adic modular forms, Galois representations, and Iwasawa theory. The problems I am currently working on are about Fourier coefficients of the p-adic generalized Hecke eigenform coming from a CM modular form of critical slope, and about ramifications over the weight space of eigenvarieties of overconvergent Hilbert modular forms.
