2020.09.14
In general, there is the construction of Eisenstein cohomology. Can we make use of this and integral formula of L-functions to construct (rational) candidate of Euler system classes? The general strategy to prove tame norm relation using integral formula is to first unfold the local zeta integral using Whittacker models, then to find a suitable Hecke element so that the unfolded sum is equal to 1 at the Hecke element acting on the spherical vector (the unfolded sum is equal to L-value at the spherical vector).
